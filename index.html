TechLancaster

Supporting the tech community in Lancaster.

  • TechLancaster started as a simple local meetup, a safe place for like-mined tech people to come together and geek out.
  • We've strived to be open, welcoming, and supportive to everyone in the community regardless of their interest or background.
  • The past year has seen amazing growth in Lancaster's tech scene, and we beleive this is only the beginning. As members of the tech community, we want to support this growth and ensure the community remains a heathly, open, and welcoming place for all its current and future members.
